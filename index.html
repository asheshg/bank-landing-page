Frequently asked questions

How do I open a bank account?

You can start online or visit a branch with identity proof, address proof, PAN or Form 60, and a recent photograph.

What should I do if my card is lost?

Block the card immediately through mobile banking, internet banking, customer care, or your nearest branch, then request a replacement.

How can I reset internet banking access?

Use the bank's official online banking portal to reset your password, or visit a branch if additional verification is needed.

How do I report an unauthorised transaction?

Report it to the bank as soon as possible through official customer care, mobile banking, or a branch, and keep the complaint reference number.
